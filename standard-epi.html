What is ePI?

Drug Labelling, Digitized

ePI transforms the way product information is authored, regulated, and consumed — from static PDFs to structured, machine-readable FHIR documents that update in real time.

📋

Structured Content

SmPC sections, package leaflets, and labelling content encoded as FHIR Compositions with coded sections, enabling granular access and search.

🌍

Multilingual by Design

Support for multiple languages and jurisdictional variations within a single ePI document — one product, many markets, one source of truth.

🔄

Always Current

Version-controlled documents with tracked changes. When a regulator approves a label change, it propagates instantly to all connected systems.

🏥

Clinical Integration

EHR systems, pharmacy platforms, and clinical decision support tools can consume ePI data directly — enabling contextual drug information at the point of care.

🤖

AI & Automation Ready

Structured FHIR data enables automated comparison, translation, conflict detection, and intelligent search across product information.

📱

Patient Accessible

Present leaflet content in patient-friendly digital formats — accessible, responsive, and available in the patient's own language.

Why Implement ePI?

Transform Your Labelling Workflow

Organizations implementing ePI report faster regulatory submissions, reduced errors, and seamless global label management.

✓
80% Faster Label Updates

Push structured changes directly to regulators — no more reformatting PDFs for each jurisdiction.

✓
Single Source of Truth

Maintain one authoritative ePI document per product. Regional variations inherit from the core.

✓
Automated Compliance

FHIR validation ensures structural and terminological conformance before submission.

✓
Real-Time Patient Safety

Safety-critical label changes reach healthcare providers and patients the moment they're approved.
