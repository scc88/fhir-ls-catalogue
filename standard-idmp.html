What is IDMP?

Product Identity, Standardized

IDMP provides a single, globally recognized framework to name and identify pharmaceutical products, ensuring accurate product information exchange.

📋

Structured Content

SmPC sections, package leaflets, and labelling content encoded as FHIR Compositions with coded sections, enabling granular access and search.

🌍

Multilingual by Design

Support for multiple languages and jurisdictional variations within a single ePI document — one product, many markets, one source of truth.

🔄

Always Current

Version-controlled documents with tracked changes. When a regulator approves a label change, it propagates instantly to all connected systems.

🏥

Clinical Integration

EHR systems, pharmacy platforms, and clinical decision support tools can consume ePI data directly — enabling contextual drug information at the point of care.

🤖

AI & Automation Ready

Structured FHIR data enables automated comparison, translation, conflict detection, and intelligent search across product information.

📱

Patient Accessible

Present leaflet content in patient-friendly digital formats — accessible, responsive, and available in the patient's own language.

Why Implement IDMP?

Transform Your Regulatory Master Data

Organizations implementing IDMP report streamlined regulatory submissions, globally consistent product tracking, and seamless cross-border data exchange.

✓
Global Interoperability

Exchange structured substance, product, and organization data seamlessly across regulatory jurisdictions.

✓
Single Source of Truth

Maintain one authoritative IDMP graph per product. National catalogs inherit from the global core.

✓
Automated Submissions

Generate target SPOR or SPL XML payloads automatically from a unified FHIR master data layer.

✓
Enhanced Signal Detection

Standardized substance and product identifiers enable precise pharmacovigilance and recall management.
