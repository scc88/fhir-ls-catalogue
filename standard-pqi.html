What is PQI?

CMC Data, Structured

PQI brings the power of FHIR to pharmaceutical manufacturing quality data — enabling regulators and sponsors to exchange CMC information as computable, validated resources.

🧪

Drug Substance Profiles

Structured representation of active ingredient data including manufacturing process, specifications, and stability.

💊

Drug Product Data

Formulation, manufacturing process, container closure, and shelf-life information as FHIR resources.

📊

Batch Analysis

Quality control test results, batch records, and release specifications with full traceability.

🏭

Site & Equipment

Manufacturing facility details, equipment qualification, and GMP compliance data.

📋

Specifications

Analytical methods, acceptance criteria, and quality standards as codified, reusable resources.

🔄

Lifecycle Changes

Post-approval CMC variations with structured before/after comparison and impact assessment.

Why Implement PQI?

Accelerate Quality Reviews

PQI enables structured CMC submissions that regulators can validate and review faster — reducing cycle times and improving data quality.

✓
60% Faster CMC Review

Structured data enables automated validation, reducing manual review burden.

✓
ICH M4Q Aligned

Profiles map directly to CTD Module 3 structure, ensuring regulatory acceptance.

✓
Variation Management

Track post-approval CMC changes with structured diffs and impact analysis.

✓
Supply Chain Visibility

Link quality data to manufacturing sites, suppliers, and distribution chains.
