MedicinalProduct
A pharmaceutical product intended for administration to humans or animals, identified by an MPID (ISO IDMP). Core entity referenced across ePI, PQI, ICSR, and APIX.
Reference IDMP
ePI PQI ICSR APIX RTQ

RegulatoryAuthority
A national or supranational medicines regulatory agency, identified by a standardised code. Mapped to EMA OMS Organisation codes and FDA establishment identifiers.
