The Opportunity

From Documents to Data

Today, vast amounts of regulated life sciences information are locked in PDFs — SmPCs, CTDs, Adverse Event reports, Q&A letters. This creates friction, delays, and risk across the entire product lifecycle.

FHIR-LS provides a single, layered methodology to transform this ecosystem: one data model, machine-readable by default, applicable from clinical trials through post-market surveillance.

⚡
Real-Time Exchange

Push structured updates to label content, safety signals, and regulatory decisions the moment they occur.

🤖
Automated Workflows

Machine-readable formats enable straight-through processing and reduce manual data entry across the regulatory chain.

🔗
End-to-End Traceability

Link data from submission to approval to post-market action using FHIR references and persistent identifiers.

📄 Today (PDF/XML)
  • Static, unstructured documents
  • Manual re-keying of data
  • Weeks-long review cycles
  • No version traceability
  • Format-per-agency
→
🔥 With FHIR-LS
  • Structured, computable data
  • Automated ingestion & validation
  • Real-time notifications
  • Full audit trail via FHIR history
  • One standard, global reach
