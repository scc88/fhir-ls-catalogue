Health Technology Assessment

The proposed FHIR standard for structured HTA data exchange. Enable transparent, computable health technology assessments that connect to the full regulatory record — from clinical evidence and safety data to pricing and reimbursement decisions.

What is HTA?

Market Access, Data-Driven

FHIR HTA standardizes the exchange of health technology assessment data — from clinical evidence submissions to pricing and reimbursement decisions — closing the last mile of the drug lifecycle.

📋

Evidence Packages

Structured clinical evidence, comparator data, and outcomes research in FHIR-native formats for HTA body review.

💰

Pricing & Reimbursement

Codified pricing proposals, budget impact analyses, and reimbursement decision data.

🏛️

Multi-Agency

Support for joint clinical assessments under the EU HTA Regulation and multi-country procedures.

📊

Real-World Evidence

Integration of RWE and patient-reported outcomes data into the assessment dossier.

🔗

Linked to ICSR & ePI

Cross-reference safety data and labelling information directly within the HTA submission.

⏱️

Expedited Access

Structured submissions accelerate HTA review timelines — getting therapies to patients faster.

Why Implement HTA?

Close the Last Mile

Today, HTA submissions are document-heavy and disconnected from the regulatory record. FHIR HTA connects the dots — from authorization to access.

✓
EU HTA Regulation Ready

Align with the 2025 EU HTA Regulation requirements for joint clinical assessments.

✓
Connected Evidence

Link HTA evidence to the same FHIR resources used in the MAA and safety reporting.

✓
Transparent Decisions

Structured assessment outputs enable reproducible, auditable reimbursement decisions.

✓
Cross-Border Collaboration

Share HTA data across multiple assessment bodies using a common format.
