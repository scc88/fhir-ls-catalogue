What is RTQ?

Regulatory Q&A, Structured

RTQ digitizes the question-and-response process that occurs during regulatory assessment — replacing untracked email threads with structured, auditable FHIR-based exchanges.

📬

Structured Questions

Regulators issue questions as coded FHIR resources — linked to specific dossier sections, ePI content, or CMC data points.

📝

Linked Responses

Sponsor responses reference the exact data elements under question, with attached evidence and updated FHIR resources.

⏱️

Deadline Tracking

Built-in due dates, priority levels, and clock-stop management aligned with regulatory procedure timelines.

🔗

Cross-Domain References

Questions can reference ePI sections, PQI data, ICSR cases, or any FHIR resource in the regulatory dossier.

📊

Assessment Workflows

Support for rapporteur/co-rapporteur workflows, consolidated question lists, and multi-agency procedures.

🔍

Full Audit Trail

Every question, response, and follow-up is versioned and auditable — complete transparency for all parties.

Why Implement RTQ?

Faster Assessments, Better Outcomes

RTQ eliminates the communication gaps that delay regulatory reviews — ensuring questions reach the right experts and responses address exactly what was asked.

✓
50% Shorter Q&A Cycles

Structured questions with linked references eliminate ambiguity and reduce back-and-forth.

✓
No Lost Questions

Every question is tracked, assigned, and has a clear response deadline — nothing falls through the cracks.

✓
Connected to the Dossier

Questions link directly to submission data — reviewers and sponsors see the same source of truth.

✓
Multi-Agency Ready

Support for reliance and work-sharing procedures across multiple regulatory bodies.
